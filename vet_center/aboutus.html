About Us

Mission

Offer well-being both to animals and to the families of our patients through the provision of veterinary and complementary medical services, delivering quality and satisfaction, exceeding the expectations of our clients, contributing to the innovation and professional development of the Veterinary Medical sector of the Region. Thus improving the quality of life of our patients and their families, creating the appropriate conditions of health and well-being, maintaining a constant teaching to the owners for the maintenance of their pets.

Vision

To be undisputed leaders in veterinary and complementary medical services, to provide our clients with quality and professionalism, with excellent facilities, a highly qualified medical team and a continuing education department with local, national and international recognition.

Get to know our professionals better

We are a company that provides nursing services with more than 6 years of experience, made up of a select group of qualified professionals to provide a service with high quality standards in the hospital and home environment. Our commitment is to ensure the well-being of our patients, contributing to a better quality of life, as well as taking care of our work team, which makes it possible for the Vet Center family to be bigger every day. More than 50 professionals work at the Vet Center with exclusive dedication, with the aim of offering the patient the best possible medical and human treatment, we are passionate about what we do. Thanks to this model, our specialists distribute their time between three tasks that we consider essential for excellent care and continuous improvement. In this way, we ensure constant training and professional development, which results in benefits for our patients.
